How to Play

🎯 Goal — Wipe out the enemy. A side is only defeated once it has no settlements and fewer than 5 units left — enough survivors can march somewhere fertile and rebuild.

🧍 Units — One unit type, three switchable roles: Deploy fights, Supply hauls food and runs supply routes, Farmer grows food working a settlement's fields. Switch roles any time from the selection panel.

🌾 Food — Every unit eats. Armies stay fed inside your own territory; beyond it, toggle Pillage to live off the land you're on, or run a Supply route from a settlement. Hungry armies fight worse — and starve at zero.

🏠 Settlements — Cost 5 units to found; build on fertile land. Each runs one mode: Farm (grow population), train Supply/Deploy units, or Stockpile food. A garrisoned town defends its walls hard; an empty one falls in seconds.

🕹️ Controls — Touch: tap a blob to select, tap ground to move, tap an enemy to attack; tap your selection again for actions (Build, Pillage, Split…). Desktop: left-click selects, right-click moves or attacks, drag box-selects, wheel zooms.

Everything else — sieges, flanking, raiding caravans — you'll pick up as you play.
